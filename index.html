Offline
The Series

A found footage series built from the ground up — written, directed, and produced by Raymond. Offline follows the story of Jay and the antagonist Phase across six episodes of escalating tension, mystery, and raw unfiltered storytelling.

Shot in the found footage format with a detailed visual bible, voiceover maps, character arcs, and social media tie-ins that blur the line between fiction and reality.
